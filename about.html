About Us

Uphawu Metallurgical Services (UMS) functions as a materials testing and consultancy institution. We conduct destructive and non- destructive testing, provide metallurgical advice and consultancy to ensure improved production of quality products. UMS also serves as a supplier of various industrial commodities across various sectors, these including safety wear and general engineering consumables. This wing of the company has shown significant growth since its inception and continues to grow from strength to strength.

UMS also operates in the Human resource management space. We have seen growth in this aspect of our establishment and continue to improve our service to our clients.

Our Mission

Make a meaningful contribution in the engineering sector by providing a quality and reliable service.
Provide an unparalleled industrial supplies service.
Ensure growth of our establishment by keeping  abreast of the latest developments in technology.
Inculcate a principle of skills transfer by means of  training and mentorship programmes

Uphawu Metallurgical Services (PTY) LTD was established in 2013.

The company began as a sole mechanical testing service provider and later expanded into other avenues of material testing as well as industrial supplies.
